I am interested in exploring data from the American Community Survey from the U.S. Census revolving around voting patterns and race and ethnicity. The dataset includes information regarding state, race and ethnicity, total estimated population (rounded) and its margin of error, and citizenship status for U.S. citizens in that geographic area and group (rounded) and its margin of error.

Thus, my research question is as follows: What is the relationship between the Latinos and Asians who are able to vote?

dependent variable: proportion of citizens ind. variable: total population control: proportion over 18 (CVAP)

cvap_data <- state |>
  pivot_wider(
    names_from = LNTITLE,
    values_from = c(TOT_EST, ADU_EST, CIT_EST, CVAP_EST)
  ) |>
  mutate(CVAP_prop_Latino = CVAP_EST_Latino/TOT_EST_Latino) |>
  mutate(CVAP_prop_Asian = CVAP_EST_Asian/TOT_EST_Asian)
